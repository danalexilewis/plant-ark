# Commercial licensing

Plant Ark is published under the [Prosperity Public License 3.0.0](LICENSE) — free for non-commercial use, with a 30-day commercial trial. After the trial, **commercial use requires a purchased license from us.**

## What counts as commercial use

Anything not covered by the free terms in the [LICENSE](LICENSE). The license is free for:

- Personal, hobby, study, and research use
- Charities, schools, public research, and government bodies
- Contributions back to this project under a standard open license

A 30-day honor-system trial is included so commercial users can evaluate before buying. One trial per company, not per person.

Commercial use beyond the trial includes, for example:

- Selling Plant Ark kits, modules, or assembled units
- Manufacturing or retailing products derived from these specs or firmware
- A paid service, SaaS, or app built on Plant Ark designs or the PlantBus protocol
- Paid consulting or contract work based on this project
- OEM / white-label use in another commercial product

## How to obtain a commercial license

1. Describe your intended use (product, volume, geography, timeline).
2. Contact the Plant Ark rights holders:
   - Open a GitHub issue with the label `commercial-license`, or
   - Email the maintainers (contact to be published at retail launch).
3. We will respond with license terms and pricing.

No trademark license is granted by either the Prosperity license or a commercial license. "Plant Ark" and related marks remain the property of the rights holders.
