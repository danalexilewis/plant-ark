# Non-Goals for V1

Explicit exclusions from Plant Ark v1. Do not specify, design, or implement these until a future phase.

## Product features

- Cloud account system or remote access beyond Meshtastic
- Native mobile app (local web UI is sufficient)
- AI plant diagnosis or recommendations
- Camera / computer vision monitoring
- Voice assistant integration

## Water treatment

- Ozone treatment
- Open UV-C exposure
- Direct mains water connection (auto-fill)
- Complex pH/EC dosing automation
- Peristaltic precision dosing pumps

## Electrical / hardware

- Mains wiring inside custom wet enclosure
- DIY grow light drivers with mains inside tent
- Full product-grade PCB before bench prototype validates design
- Full CAD perfection before water test
- Latching solenoid valves (v2 low-power variant)

## Software

- Full binary PlantBus encoding (JSON acceptable for v1 simulator)
- Home Assistant integration (optional later)
- Grafana dashboards (optional later)
- MQTT broker as required dependency
- Over-the-air firmware updates

## Scale

- More than 2 irrigation modules in first prototype
- Multi-shelf / multi-tray cart (single tray for v1)
- Commercial product certification (CE/UL) — future phase
